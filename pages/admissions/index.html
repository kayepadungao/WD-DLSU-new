Entrance Examination Process:

The DLSU Entrance Examination (DLSU-EE) assesses the academic aptitude of students through various sections including mathematics, language proficiency, and general knowledge. Upon passing, students will receive an admission letter via email.

Graduate Admissions

DLSU also offers a comprehensive graduate school program aimed at developing leaders in various fields. Our Master's and Doctoral programs are designed to cater to professionals looking to expand their expertise.

Application Requirements:

  • Completed graduate application form
  • Transcript of records from previous institutions
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Personal Statement
  • Application fee

Graduate Programs Offered:

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Science in Engineering
  • Doctor of Philosophy in Educational Leadership
  • Doctor of Business Administration (DBA)

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We believe in making education accessible to deserving students. DLSU offers a wide variety of scholarships, including:

  • Merit-based Scholarships
  • Need-based Financial Assistance
  • External Scholarships

Financial aid and scholarship applications are available through the Admissions Office and online portal.

Application Process

Follow these steps to apply to DLSU:

  1. Fill out the online application form available on the DLSU admissions portal.
  2. Submit all required documents including your birth certificate, high school diploma, and 2x2 photo.
  3. Pay the application fee via the online payment system.
  4. Take the entrance exam (DLSU-EE) and wait for the results.
  5. After receiving your admission letter, proceed to enrollment.
